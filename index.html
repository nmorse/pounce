Pounce

Pounce is a programming language with roots going back to FORTH, Joy and Cat. In the category of stack based, concatenative programming languages, Pounce aims to be simple, accessable and portable.

Give Pounce a try, here in the browser or experiment with Pounce in Python. this Repo contains simple interperetors that let you try out the surprising power of concatenative programming, jump in and expand your thinking about what a programming language is.
